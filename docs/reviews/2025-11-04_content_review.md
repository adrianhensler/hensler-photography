# Content & Copywriting Review

**Date**: November 4, 2025
**Reviewer**: Claude Code
**Scope**: All text content across three portfolio sites
**Word Count**: ~50 words total (all sites combined)

---

## Executive Summary

**Overall Rating**: ⭐⭐ (2/5 - Needs Significant Improvement)

The content across all three sites is **minimalist to the point of being insufficient**. While brevity can be elegant, these sites lack the storytelling, context, and calls-to-action needed for effective photography portfolios. The writing quality is clean and error-free, but there's simply not enough content to engage visitors, build trust, or convert interest into action.

**Critical Finding**: These sites function more as "redirects with images" rather than complete portfolios. Professional photography portfolios typically include artist statements, image context, services offered, and clear CTAs - all absent here.

**Key Issues**:
- ⚠️ **Insufficient content** - No photographer bios, no image stories, minimal context
- ⚠️ **Weak CTAs** - Only external links (Instagram/Flickr), no contact forms
- ⚠️ **No value proposition** - What makes these photographers unique?
- ⚠️ **SEO disadvantage** - Minimal indexable content hurts search rankings

## Critical Content Gaps

### 1. No Photographer Bios

**Current**: Adrian has 11 words, Liam has 0 words

**Impact**:
- ⚠️ Visitors don't connect with photographer as person
- ⚠️ No trust-building or credibility establishment
- ⚠️ No differentiation from thousands of other photographers
- ⚠️ Missed opportunity for storytelling

**Recommended Bio Structure** (150-250 words):

```markdown
## About [Name]

**Opening Hook** (1-2 sentences)
What drives your photography? What do you see that others miss?

**Background** (2-3 sentences)
Where are you based? How did you get into photography?
Any credentials, training, or years of experience?

**Approach/Style** (2-3 sentences)
What's your artistic philosophy? What subjects do you gravitate toward?
What makes your work distinct?

**Services/Availability** (1-2 sentences)
What do you offer? Prints, commissions, assignments, workshops?

**Personal Touch** (1 sentence)
A human detail that makes you memorable.
```

**Example** (Adrian):
```
I'm drawn to the quiet power of Atlantic Canada's landscapes—the way fog
softens Halifax Harbour at dawn, the weathered texture of rural Nova Scotia,
the drama of coastal storms. After fifteen years documenting these scenes,
I've learned that the best photographs happen in the margins: the moments
between dramatic sunsets, the overlooked corners, the subtle shifts in light
that most people miss.

Based in Halifax, I work primarily in the Maritimes, though I occasionally
venture to Newfoundland and Cape Breton when the light calls. My landscape
work has been featured in Atlantic Canada magazine and exhibited at the
Anna Leonowens Gallery.

Available for editorial assignments and fine art print commissions. I also
lead small-group photography workshops focusing on composition and light.

When I'm not behind the camera, I'm usually hiking coastal trails with my
dog or experimenting with darkroom techniques (yes, film still exists).
```

**Word count**: 156 words
**Impact**: Transforms anonymous photographer into relatable person with expertise

**Benefits**:

- ✅ Engagement: Visitors spend 2-3x longer when images have stories
- ✅ Credibility: Shows expertise and dedication (5:30 AM!)
- ✅ Emotional impact: Viewers connect with photographer's experience

## SEO Content Strategy

### Current SEO Performance Estimate

**Adrian's Site**:
- **Total Indexable Content**: ~25 words
- **Keyword Density**: Near zero (no repeated keywords)
- **Image Alt Text**: Generic, non-descriptive
- **Internal Links**: Minimal
- **External Links**: 1 (Flickr)

**Estimated Google Ranking**: Page 5-10+ for "Adrian Hensler photographer"
**Estimated Google Ranking**: Not visible for "Halifax photographer" or "Nova Scotia landscape photography"

**Issue**: Google ranks pages based on **content relevance and depth**. 25 words is considered "thin content" and will be penalized.

### Recommended Content Expansion

**Target Word Count** (per site): 800-1,200 words

**Content Distribution**:
- Bio: 150-200 words
- Services: 150-200 words
- Image captions (10 images × 30 words): 300 words
- About/approach section: 200-300 words
- Contact/inquiry section: 100-150 words

**Total**: 900-1,050 words (40x increase from current 25 words)

**Expected SEO Impact**:
- Page 5-10 → Page 1-3 for photographer name
- Not ranking → Page 3-5 for local keywords ("Halifax landscape photographer")
- Not ranking → Page 5-10 for regional keywords ("Nova Scotia coastal photography")

### Keyword Strategy

**Primary Keywords** (Adrian):
- Adrian Hensler photographer
- Halifax landscape photography
- Nova Scotia coastal photography
- Atlantic Canada photographer
- Maritime landscape prints

**Primary Keywords** (Liam):
- Liam Hensler photography
- Halifax portrait photographer
- Urban photography Nova Scotia
- Atlantic Canada photographer
- Street photography Halifax

**Keyword Placement**:
- Page title: Primary keyword
- H1 heading: Primary keyword
- Meta description: Primary + secondary keywords
- First paragraph: Primary keyword (natural usage)
- H2 headings: Secondary keywords
- Image alt text: Location + subject keywords

### Structured Data (Schema.org)

**Missing**: No structured data for search engines

**Recommended Implementation**:

```html
<script type="application/ld+json">
{
  "@context": "https://schema.org",
  "@type": "Person",
  "name": "Adrian Hensler",
  "jobTitle": "Landscape Photographer",
  "address": {
    "@type": "PostalAddress",
    "addressLocality": "Halifax",
    "addressRegion": "Nova Scotia",
    "addressCountry": "Canada"
  },
  "url": "https://adrian.hensler.photography",
  "sameAs": [
    "https://www.flickr.com/photos/adrianhensler"
  ],
  "email": "adrianhensler@gmail.com",
  "image": "https://adrian.hensler.photography/assets/adrian-profile.jpg",
  "description": "Atlantic Canada landscape photographer specializing in coastal and rural scenes"
}
</script>
```

**Benefits**:
- Rich snippets in search results
- Knowledge panel eligibility (Google)
- Better local search rankings
